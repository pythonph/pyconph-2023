We use the funds from patrons to offer Financial Aid to attendees.

We recognize that regardless of price, conference tickets can be a challenge for some folks. In an effort to make this conference accessible to everyone, we offer financial aid for attendees who wish to attend but otherwise would not be able to. Anyone hoping to attend the conference can apply, although grants awards are prioritized for speakers and main contributors.
